Virtual CFO Services

Scale Your Business, Not Your Overhead.

You know how to run your business. We know how to engineer its financial growth.

As your company scales, basic bookkeeping and annual compliance are no longer enough. You need strategic foresight, cash flow optimization, and data-driven insights to make high-stakes decisions. But hiring a full-time, in-house Chief Financial Officer is a massive overhead expense.

That’s where we step in. Our Virtual CFO services deliver executive-level financial strategy, backed by modern automation, at a fraction of the cost of a traditional hire.

Why Your Business Needs a Virtual CFO

Many businesses hit a growth ceiling because their financial systems can't keep up with their operational success. We bridge that gap. If you are experiencing rapid growth, struggling with unpredictable cash flow, or navigating complex tax landscapes, a vCFO translates your raw data into an actionable roadmap.

Our Virtual CFO Offerings

We don't just look at what happened last quarter; we project what will happen next year.

Strategic Financial Forecasting & Modeling

We build robust financial models that allow you to test scenarios before you make a move. Whether you are planning an expansion, a new product line, or seeking funding, we map out the financial impact.

Cash Flow Management & Optimization

Revenue is vanity; cash flow is reality. We implement tight cash flow monitoring and runway analysis so you never have to guess if you can make payroll or invest in that next big opportunity.

Tech-Driven Financial Reporting

Say goodbye to waiting weeks for outdated spreadsheets. We leverage modern automation to pull, process, and present your data. You get clean, intuitive dashboards and KPIs exactly when you need them.

Profitability & Cost Analysis

We dissect your margins, identifying where you are making the most money and where you are bleeding capital. We help you cut the fat and double down on what works.

Fundraising & Board Support

Preparing for a capital raise? We get your financials investor-ready, help you determine your valuation, and stand by your side during critical stakeholder meetings.

The Saraogi Manav and Co. Advantage

Accounting shouldn't be a purely historical exercise. At Saraogi Manav and Co., we blend traditional financial expertise with a modern, technology-first approach.

We utilize advanced tools and automated workflows to strip away the busywork, allowing us to focus entirely on high-level strategy and advisory. When you partner with us, you aren't just getting an accountant; you are getting a dedicated financial co-pilot who is as invested in your bottom line as you are.
