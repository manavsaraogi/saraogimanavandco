Our Core Income Tax Services

Tailored, tech-driven tax solutions designed for the modern regulatory environment.

01

Strategic Compliance & Filing Under the New Act

We ensure your filings are accurate, optimized, and transition seamlessly into the new 2026 regulatory framework, leveraging all eligible deductions to protect your capital.

Comprehensive ITR Filing

Tailored, error-free filing for Salaried Individuals, Specialized Professionals, Business Owners, LLPs, Companies, Trusts, NGOs, and HNIs/NRIs.

Tax Audits (The New Form 26)

Seamless execution of mandatory tax audits. We handle the preparation and filing of the newly consolidated Form 26 (which entirely replaces the legacy 3CA, 3CB, and 3CD forms) for businesses crossing turnover thresholds.

Essential Certifications & Upgraded Forms

Expert handling of the newly renumbered statutory forms, ensuring zero compliance friction under the updated tax structures:

•

Form 121: (Formerly 15G/15H) Complete management for TDS non-deduction declarations.

•

Form 141: The new consolidated challan replacing 26QB, 26QC, etc., for TDS on property purchases, rent, and virtual digital assets.

•

Form 145/146: (Formerly 15CA/15CB) Required for seamless and compliant foreign remittances.

•

Forms 168 & 130: Tracking your updated annual tax statements (formerly 26AS) and managing new TDS reporting structures (formerly Form 16).

02

Litigation, Appeals & Dispute Resolution

When tax authorities raise questions, we provide strong, meticulous representation to minimize your tax liability and protect your rights at every stage.

Notice Replies & Department Liaison

Drafting factually airtight, legally sound responses to intimations, notices, and faceless assessments. We handle all direct liaison and technical correspondence with Income Tax officers to resolve discrepancies efficiently.

Appellate Representation

End-to-end drafting of Statements of Facts and Grounds of Appeal. We provide formidable, research-backed legal representation before the Commissioner of Income Tax (Appeals) and the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT).

Search, Seizure & Assessments

In the rare event of a tax raid or intense scrutiny operations, we provide immediate legal guidance. Our team rigorously reconciles seized materials, technically explains underlying assets, and fiercely safeguards your procedural rights throughout the entire assessment lifecycle.
