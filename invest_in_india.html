Capitalize on the World's Fastest-Growing Economy

Seamless market entry and rigorous regulatory compliance for global enterprises.

As the global economic center of gravity shifts, India presents an unparalleled landscape for expansion. Driven by sweeping regulatory reforms, a robust digital infrastructure, and a massive demographic dividend, the Indian market offers immense scalability for forward-thinking investors.

However, entering this dynamic ecosystem requires foresight. Navigating the intersections of local taxation, foreign exchange laws, and corporate compliance demands precision. Saraogi Manav and Co. acts as your localized institutional partner, engineering a frictionless entry into the Indian business landscape.

Entry Architecture

Guiding international enterprises through the complexities of the Indian market, we help determine and deploy the optimal corporate structure aligned with your objectives, capital layout, and regulatory requirements. Our core entry models include:

  • Wholly Owned Subsidiaries
  • Private and Public Limited Companies
  • Limited Liability Partnerships
  • Branch and Liaison Offices
  • Joint Ventures and Indian Entity Acquisitions

Lifecycle Advisory and Execution

We provide a comprehensive, unified approach to market penetration, capital structuring, and statutory governance through the following lifecycle services:

Corporate Formation

Designing tax-efficient business vehicles tailored precisely to your operational model and specific industry regulations.

Foreign Exchange and Regulatory Approvals

Navigating Reserve Bank of India mandates and maintaining strict adherence to Foreign Exchange Management Act guidelines to secure and legitimize your Foreign Direct Investments.

Expatriate and Non-Resident Advisory

Specialized tax planning for Non-Resident Indians and global assignees, focusing on seamless fund repatriation, Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement benefits, and localized income reporting.

International Taxation and Transfer Pricing

Safeguarding cross-border transactions through rigorous arm’s-length benchmarking, risk mitigation, and comprehensive local documentation.

Statutory Registrations and Licensing

Accelerating your operational readiness by securing essential credentials, including Permanent Account Number, Tax Deduction and Collection Account Number, Goods and Services Tax Identification Number, Import Export Code, and localized labor law compliances.

Direct and Indirect Tax Structuring

Architecting proactive tax frameworks to minimize corporate and Goods and Services Tax liabilities while upholding uncompromised statutory compliance.

Audit, Assurance and Financial Management

Delivering robust bookkeeping, statutory audits, and continuous regulatory oversight under the Companies Act and Indian tax statutes.

Capital Flow and Transaction Advisory

Guiding international funding mechanisms, dividend distribution, and repatriation processes in strict alignment with Foreign Direct Investment policy frameworks.
