Corporate Governance
& MCA Compliance

Transform regulatory requirements into a streamlined, automated process that protects your directors and empowers your business.

Navigating the complexities of the Companies Act and the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) portal requires absolute precision. A single missed deadline or incorrectly filed form can lead to heavy penalties, director disqualifications, and severe operational roadblocks.

Whether you are a fast-growing startup, an established Private Limited Company, or transitioning into a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), we manage your entire corporate secretarial lifecycle. We handle the heavy lifting of statutory compliance, allowing your board to focus entirely on scaling the business.

Our Corporate & Secretarial Services

We offer a 360-degree suite of corporate compliance services, from initial entity structuring to rigorous annual reporting and event-based filings.

01

Entity Formation & Structuring

We don't just register companies; we help you choose the right vehicle for your business goals and handle the entire bureaucratic process seamlessly.

Incorporation Services

End-to-end registration for Private Limited Companies, Public Limited Companies, Section 8 (NGOs), and Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs).

Initial Registrations

Procurement of Digital Signature Certificates (DSC), Director Identification Numbers (DIN), and seamless allocation of PAN/TAN and specific AO codes during the incorporation process.

02

Annual ROC Filings & Secretarial Records

Stay off the default lists. We ensure your routine corporate filings are flawlessly executed and filed on time.

Mandatory Annual Returns

Accurate preparation and uploading of essential financial and structural forms, including AOC-4 (Financial Statements) and MGT-7/MGT-7A (Annual Returns).

LLP Compliance

Timely filing of LLP Form 8 (Statement of Account & Solvency) and Form 11 (Annual Return) to maintain an active and compliant status.

Corporate Secretarial Governance

Professional drafting and maintenance of statutory registers, Board Meeting minutes, General Meeting resolutions, and attendance records to ensure you are always audit-ready.

03

Statutory Audits & Financial Reporting

Trust in your numbers is paramount for investors, banks, and stakeholders.

Independent Statutory Audits

Comprehensive, unbiased auditing of your financial statements conducted by our CA experts in strict accordance with the Companies Act and Indian Accounting Standards.

XBRL Filings

Expert conversion, validation, and filing of financial statements in XBRL format for mandated companies to ensure exact compliance with MCA requirements.

04

Event-Based Compliances & Director Advisory

Businesses evolve, and so does their structure. We guide you through complex corporate transitions and structural changes.

Board & Shareholder Management

Legal advisory and form filings for the appointment, resignation, or removal of Directors and Auditors, as well as mandatory DIR-3 KYC compliance.

Capital & Structural Changes

Navigating the legalities of altering authorized share capital, issuing or allotting new shares (PAS-3), processing rights issues, and private placements.

Registered Office Shifts

Managing the legal protocols and MCA approvals required when strategically moving your registered office within the same state or across state lines.

Company Closure

Streamlined, rapid processing for the formal strike-off and closure of dormant or inactive companies (Form STK-2) to cleanly dissolve liabilities.
